Wedding newspaper

A newspaper I designed for my brother's wedding. In collaboration with friends and family of the future spouses we collect pictures, stories and interview texts, which I assembled in a layout.

Since the production was financed by friends themselves, the contents had to be as compact as possible without leaving out any of the material gathered.

The product has 28 pages printed on 7 sheets of satin-finished digital printing paper.
